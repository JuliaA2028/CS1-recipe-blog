CuzCuz Doce


Description

A coconut treat. It’s a soft, a little sweet, and perfect with butter or condensed milk on top.

ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal (flocão de milho)
  • ½ cup grated coconut
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix cornmeal, coconut, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add milk until the texture feels a bit moist
  3. Let it sit for 10 minutes to absorb.
  4. Steam for about 10–15 minutes, until firm and fluffy.
  5. Serve with drizzle of condensed milk..

Brigadeiro


Description

Brazil’s favorite party treat! It’s like a chocolate truffle, and covered in sprinkles. Super easy and only three ingredients.

ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for rolling)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, mix condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until thick and pulling from the sides.
  3. Let cool!
  4. Roll into small balls and coat with chocolate sprinkles!
